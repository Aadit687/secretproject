June 2026 · From me, to you

My love,

I don't always know how to say things out loud. You know this. But I wanted to find a way to show you everything I carry for you — so I built this.

Two years. It sounds like a small number until you think about everything that lives inside of it. Every road trip. Every stupid argument we actually got through. Every time one of us was falling apart and the other one just showed up. That's not nothing. That's everything.

I know things haven't always been easy. I know we've both been tired sometimes — of the world, of the distance between where we are and where we want to be. But here's what I know for certain: I would rather be tired with you than rested without you.

You are the funniest person I've ever met, which is embarrassing for me because you don't even try. You're the person I want to tell things to first. You're the person I think about when something good happens and also when something bad happens. You're just the person.

We're going to make it. Not because things will be perfect, but because we love each other even when we're both running on empty. That kind of love doesn't quit. And neither do we.

This website is just a small attempt to put everything into one place — all our photos, all our memories, all the reasons I fall more in love with you every single day. You deserve a whole museum. For now, you get this.

Forever yours ♡
